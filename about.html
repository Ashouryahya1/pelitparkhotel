ABOUT PELIT PARK HOTEL

A Story of Hospitality and Heart

Pelit Park Hotel was born from a passion for welcoming guests with warmth and creating a home away from home in the heart of Trabzon.

Our Journey

Since opening its doors, Pelit Park Hotel has stood as a boutique destination where modern comfort meets the charm of the Black Sea coast. Designed with thoughtful touches and tailored services, every stay reflects our commitment to hospitality.

We are proud to welcome travelers from all over the world and share the vibrancy of Trabzon. Whether you visit for business or leisure, we believe every guest deserves to feel relaxed, connected, and cared for.

MEET OUR FOUNDER

Ali Şahin

Founder & Visionary Host

Ali Şahin envisioned Pelit Park Hotel as more than a place to stay. Inspired by the warmth of Trabzon’s people, he set out to create a haven where every visitor is welcomed like family.

His attention to detail, love for the city, and dedication to service guide our team every day. With multilingual staff and thoughtfully designed spaces, Ali ensures that each guest enjoys a seamless and memorable experience.

Our Goals at Pelit Park Hotel

Celebrate Trabzon’s Spirit

From local flavors to cultural experiences, we showcase the heritage of our city so guests can connect with the authentic character of the region.

Deliver Thoughtful Comfort

Every room and service is designed to feel personal—from spacious suites to 24/7 reception—so you can unwind with ease.

Build Lasting Relationships

We believe in genuine hospitality. Our goal is to create long-term connections with guests who return to Pelit Park Hotel year after year.
